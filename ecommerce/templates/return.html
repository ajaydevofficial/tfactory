Return Policy


We only accept return to the orders in which item delivered is damaged or the items which are wrongly delivered. The decisions regarding the return will be completely belongs to T Factory. Received Incorrect or Damaged goods? If case the box in which the item has arrived has extensive visible damage, you are requested to contact our customer support as soon as possible at info@tfactorylive.com . We will ask you to return the product to us and arrange a courier back. Your money will be refunded in full within 20 working days of the receipt of the product.
